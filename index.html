Made by Hand · Made with Heart

Every piece on this shelf was made by someone who refused to be counted out.

HasthKala is a small marketplace for bookmarks, paintings, and handicrafts created entirely by differently-abled artisans. Each product you bring home is a wage earned, a skill honoured, and proof that talent has never needed permission. When you buy here, you're not just decorating a shelf — you're funding someone's independence.

Our Promise

A craft economy where ability is measured in skill, patience and imagination — not in what the world assumes you can't do.

Fair Pay, Always

Every artisan is paid fairly and directly for the time, care and skill that goes into each piece — no middlemen, no discounts on dignity.

Tools & Training

A share of every sale goes back into materials, accessible workspaces and skill-building, so creators can grow at their own pace.

One Piece, One Story
